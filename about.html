About

Story

Hi! I'm Sidney Torck. I'm a UI Designer and an artist with a passion for story telling.

Coming from the video game field, I studied the production of visual assets for games and always had an affinity towards concept art, illustration, and interfaces. I worked on different games where I had responsibilities like drawing concept art for characters, environment, and props, modeling and texture 3D assets for game environments, and drawing 2D assets for the environment of a side-scroller game. I drew the user interface for most of the games I worked on and this is why I decided to make the integrate UI design.

My project collaborations taught me to listen to my team's needs and provide support where needed by taking initiative and learning new skills the team was lacking. I learned to follow a strict artistic direction and style guide. I even help define artistic direction with my concept art. I particularly enjoy understanding the user's needs and offering them an interface that is creative and surprising, yet straightforward and easy to use.

In my free time, I enjoy playing table top games with friends. I master Dungeons and Dragons and other games. My practice of Chito-Ryu Karate (Black belt, 1st dan) since the age of five. This taught me discipline and mental clarity.
